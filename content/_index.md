---
title: "Vaults of the ODD"
---

Vaults of the ODD is the personal publishing home of Drew Griffiths. The name describes the structure: each section is a vault of something odd and eclectic, kept apart so you can wander into whichever one suits you.

It began with close readings of TSR-era Dungeons & Dragons and it now gathers everything alongside that, from gaming analysis to fiction, mythology, and writing on Welsh constitutional questions, together with a growing range of published journals.
