Writing on Welsh constitutional development, independence and governance. The papers below form a connected body of work from 2026, written as an independent contribution to Welsh constitutional thinking. They share a single thesis — that constitutional durability follows institutional capability rather than preceding it — and they are best read in the order below, beginning with the Framework and moving outward to its companions. Each paper is fully readable here, and downloadable as its original typeset PDF.
