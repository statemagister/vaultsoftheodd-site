## How this connects

This is the methodological companion of the set. It can be read entirely on its own, but it underpins the rest: the careful, domain-by-domain discipline it sets out — keeping legal possibility, scholarly disagreement, practical likelihood, and political strategy distinct — is the same neutral method that lets [**A Framework for Welsh Constitutional Development**](/politics/welsh-constitutional-development-framework/) and its companions describe contested ground without arguing a conclusion.

## Sources

The full references are in the PDF. The paper's principal legal anchors include the International Court of Justice's 2010 advisory opinion on the [Kosovo declaration of independence](https://www.icj-cij.org/case/141), James Crawford's *The Creation of States in International Law*, and Article 4 of the [UN Charter](https://www.un.org/en/about-us/un-charter/full-text).
